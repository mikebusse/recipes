MmmMmm, Guacamole!!

For this recipe, you will need:

8 Avocados
Pinch of Salt
Half a Lemon
1/4 cup salsa

1. Peel the Avocados and put the meat into a bowl, take out the seed.

2. Add the 1/4 cup of Salsa.

3. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon on top of the avocado and tomato salsa.

4. Add salt to taste.

5. Enjoy!!
